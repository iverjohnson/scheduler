Community Paramedic Scheduling
 
 
 
    Instructions:
  • 1. Locate date using mini-calendar or arrows
  • 2. Double click on the requested start time in the row of the appropriate CP
  • 3. Enter patients initials, ignore the description box, choose CP-Initial or CP-Follow-up, and select the appropriate end time
  • 4. Click save button.
  • 5. Be sure information is entered into RightCAD.
  • To change or delete an appointment, double click it.
    Notes:
  • Check EMS Manager for on-duty Community Paramedic
  • Do not schedule appointment start times before 0900
  • Do not schedule appointment start times later than 1500
  • CP-Initial Visits are 90 minutes
  • CP-Follow-up Visits are 60 minutes
